Certification Framework

Certification Levels

Progressive standards for responsible mining

1
Registered
Entry-level registration. Basic documentation submitted. Beginning the certification journey.
2
Trace Initiated
Supply chain tracing has begun. Preliminary data collection. Self-reported compliance metrics.
3
Verified Practices
Third-party verification of key practices. Environmental and labor standards documented.
4
Independently Audited
Full independent audit completed. ESG compliance verified. Indigenous consent documented.
5
Certified with Distinction
Excellence in all categories. Community benefit programs active. Carbon reduction targets met.
6
LCA Prestige
Full lifecycle assessment. Net-positive environmental impact. Industry leadership in responsible mining.
